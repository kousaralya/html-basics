Cooker Paneer Recipe

Cooker Paneer Recipe with detailed photo and video recipe. An extremely simple and easy paneer curry recipe prepared with basic gravy ingredients in a pressure cooker.Paneer masala curry prepared in a new way by adding all the ingredients to the cooker.

Preparation:Firstly, you can add vegetables of your choice to make a mix veg curry. Also, fry the onions to a golden brown, or else the raw smell stays in the curry.
Additionally, pressure cooking helps to soften the onion and tomato quickly.firstly, in a pressure cooker heat 2 tbsp oil and saute 1 tsp cumin until it turns aromatic. add 6 cloves of garlic and roast for a minute. further, add 3 onion and saute until the onions turn golden brown. keeping the flame on low add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp garam masala, and 1 tsp salt. saute until the spices turn aromatic. now add 3 tomato and saute for 2 minutes. add 1 cup water and mix well. cover and pressure cook for 4 whistles or until the tomato turns soft and mushy. mash the onion and tomato until it turns gravy texture. in a pan heat 1 tbsp butter, add 12 cubes paneer, ½ capsicum, and fry well. add the fried paneer and capsicum to the gravy base. also add 1 inch ginger, 1 tsp kasuri methi, and 2 tbsp coriander. mix well. finally, enjoy Cooker Paneer Recipe with roti or rice. Finally, Cooker Paneer Recipe tastes great when prepared slightly spicy and oily.
